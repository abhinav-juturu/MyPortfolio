ValueCentric Supply Chain DApp

Decentralized application for transparent pharmaceutical product tracking.

The Need for Transparency

Pharmaceutical supply chains often suffer from a lack of verifiable tracking, leading to issues with counterfeiting and accountability. This project tackled that by using **Blockchain technology** to ensure immutable records.

Key Features Implemented

  • **Product Registration:** Securely logging new products onto the blockchain.
  • **Ownership Transfer:** Transparently tracking change of hands between distributors.
  • **Verification:** Enabling end-users to verify product authenticity.

Blockchain & Web3 Stack

The DApp leverages Ethereum-based tools for decentralized operation:

**Solidity, Remix IDE, Web3.js, MetaMask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript**

The **Smart Contracts (Solidity)** enforce the rules for registration and transfer, guaranteeing immutability and security.

Enhanced Supply Chain Integrity

Impact Achieved:

Provided an enhanced level of supply chain **transparency and security**, making it virtually impossible to tamper with product origin or history.
